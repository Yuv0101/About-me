NIT SILCHAR

NIT Silchar (National Institute of Technology Silchar) is a public technical institute located in Silchar, Assam. It offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, M.Sc etc.) programs in various engineering and management fields. The B.Tech program is a 4-year course and the students are trained in their chosen branch of engineering. Admissions to the B.Tech program are done through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE Main).
